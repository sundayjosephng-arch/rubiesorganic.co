From Fresh Raw Coconut
To Refined, Nutrient-Rich Wellness Products.

Rubies Organic is a premium Nigeria-based wellness house rooted in a singular, pure intention. Beginning inside a modest family kitchen with a lone hand-selected coconut, we have gracefully grown into a certified, highly trusted source of uncompromised, naturally processed lifestyle foods.

By establishing deep, sustainable, direct-trade partnerships with independent smallholder farmers across the country, we guarantee immaculate flavor authenticity, total transparency, and continuous nutrient freshness. Our signature micro-filtered, cold-pressed coconut extractions stand out as the gold standard of natural purity.
